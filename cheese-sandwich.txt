Ingredients:
 - 2 slices bread
 - 1 tsp margarine
 - cheese
 - salad

Directions:
 1. Spread margarine on one side of each slice of bread.

 2. Place cheese and salad on the margarined side of one of the slices of bread.
 
 3. Place the other slice of bread, margarined side down, on top of the cheese and salad.
